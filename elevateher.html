ElevateHER Mentorship Program

ElevateHER is a mentorship program that is designed to support female and non-binary students who are interested in STEM or business. Each mentee will recieve personal support from a dedicated mentor at university level who will show them the major possibilities and help them develop the valuable skills that'll prepare them for college.
